About Yorba

Yorba was a non-profit free software group based in San Francisco that was active from 2009 until early 2015. This site lists the programs Yorba developed, all of which are now hosted at GNOME.

Our projects

  • Shotwell photo organizer
  • Geary email reader
  • Valencia gedit plugin for Vala
  • gexiv2 GObject wrapper around Exiv2
