Label or move a conversation

Label a conversation

Geary lets you apply one or more labels to each conversation. Geary labels correspond to labels in Gmail, or ordinary folders in other mail services.

To label one or more conversations, first select the conversation(s), then do either of the following:

  • Click the Label button on the toolbar and select a label from the resulting drop-down menu.

  • Hold down the Ctrl key and drag the conversation(s) from the conversation list to the label in the sidebar.

Move a conversation to a folder or label

To move one or more conversations to a folder or label, first select the conversation(s), then do either of the following:

  • Click the Move button on the toolbar and select a folder or label from the resulting drop-down menu.

  • Drag the conversation(s) from the conversation list to the folder or label in the sidebar.
