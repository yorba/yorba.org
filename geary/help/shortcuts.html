Keyboard shortcuts

Geary has keyboard shortcuts for most common operations.

Compose a new message

Ctrl+N or N

Reply to sender

Ctrl+R or R

Reply to all

Ctrl+Shift+R or Shift+R

Forward

Ctrl+L or F

Archive

A

Trash

Delete or Backspace

Delete

Shift+Delete or Shift+Backspace

Star

S

Unstar

D

Mark read

Ctrl+I or Shift+I

Mark unread

Ctrl+U or Shift+U

Toggle spam

Ctrl+J or !

Quit

Ctrl+Q

Zoom in

Ctrl+= or =

Zoom out

Ctrl+- or -

Reset zoom

Ctrl+0 or 0

Preferences

Ctrl+E

Accounts

Ctrl+M

Close composer window

Ctrl+W

Jump to search box

Ctrl+S

Find in current conversation

Ctrl+F

Find next in current conversation

Ctrl+G

Find previous in current conversation

Ctrl+Shift+G
